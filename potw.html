Problem of the Week

True or False: \[(\sin \theta + \cos \theta)^2 + (\sin \theta - \cos \theta)^2 \equiv 2\]

Last Week's Answer

Solution by Veronika Keras:

You put 1 stone in the first bag, and then 1 stone and the first bag in the second, and then 1 stone and the second bag in the third, etc. Each bag will have the same number of stones as its number, and you need 9 stones total.
