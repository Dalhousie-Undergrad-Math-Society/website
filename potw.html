Problem of the Week

The DUMASS hosts a party where everyone must shake hands. Once you arrive, you must either shake hands with everyone present, or with someone who has already shaken hands with everyone before taking a seat. You arrive and only the president, Nabil, shakes your hand. Every other attendee refuses to shake your hand.

Can you take a seat?
