Problem of the Week

Define a $n$-Polyomino to be a tile made of $n$ squares such that each square has an edge that touches at least one other square. A Polyomino is free if it can be freely rotated and reflected about the $x$ and $y$ axes.

What is the total number of free $5$-ominos?

Last Week's Answer

Not Possible. There are 65 tiles on our chessboard, and we only have even dominoes. We cannot make 64 or 66 tiles fit onto a 65 tile board, so it is not possible to tile our chessboard.
