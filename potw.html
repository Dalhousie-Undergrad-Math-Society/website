Problem of the Week

Is it possible to completely tile an $8 \times 8$ chess board with an extra cell attached to the first row using $1 \times 2$ dominos?.

Last Week's Answer

$$\begin{align} \sum^{n}_{k=1} 2k-1 & = 2 \sum^n_{k=1} k - \sum^n_{k=1}1\\ & = 2 \left(\frac{n(n+1)}{2}\right) - n\\ & = n(n+1) - n\\ &= n^2 \end{align}$$
