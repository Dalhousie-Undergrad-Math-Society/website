Problem of the Week

True or False: \[\sum^n_{k=1} 2k-1 = n^2\] for all $n \in \mathbb{N}$.

Last Week's Answer

The length of longest sum of consecutive n$^{\text{th}}$ powers such that \[ (x^n) + (x+1)^n + (x+2)^n + \cdots + (x + k) ^n = 2025\] is when $n = 0$. This gives us 2025 $1$'s, for a length of 2025 and sum of $2025$.
